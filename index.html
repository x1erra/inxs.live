Media Stack

Documents in.
Media out.

INXS now bundles document conversion, media downloading, and format conversion into one stack. The homepage stays simple, while the heavy lifting runs on dedicated services behind the same brand.

1
Launch the right tool from the INXS homepage without jumping between unrelated apps or repos.
2
Process files on demand using isolated backend containers for each workload: documents, downloads, and transcoding.
3
Keep it online 24/7 with Caddy, Docker volumes, and Portainer-managed restarts on the Umbrel Pi.
